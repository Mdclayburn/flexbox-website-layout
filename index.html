I was born and raised in Colorful Colorado. I love everything about my state. Especially the variety of scenery from Downtown Denver to the Rocky Mountains and then to the small "beaches" and huge lakes! I believe we've got it all.

There is just SO much for me to explore in Colorado alone it could take a lifetime to see it all. I also love how we experience all four seasons. I love the snow but they say Colorado has had more sunny days than the sunshine state itself!(Florida) Can you believe it?!

I'm sure everyone can say something good about the state they were born in but I don't think another state is as awesome as mine! Well, maybe everyone says that too. Either way I am greateful for where I was born and I cannot wait to explore and fall in love with every bit of it.
